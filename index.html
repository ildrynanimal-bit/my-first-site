Frequently Asked Questions

How does the cleaning process work?

We bring our specialized mobile cleaning truck right to your curb. Using ultra-high pressure water heated to over 200 degrees, we sanitize, deodorize, and wash away 99.9% of bacteria and grime inside and outside of your bins without using harsh chemicals.

When do you clean my bins?

We schedule your cleaning on the same day as your trash pickup day, or the day following, once your bins have been emptied by the local waste disposal service.

How do I sign up for additional cans?

When finalizing your plan, you can specify exactly how many waste, recycling, or yard debris bins you need cleaned. Additional cans are serviced at our discounted add-on rate.
